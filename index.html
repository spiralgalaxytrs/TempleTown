Is the Villa price affordable?
For approximately 14.25 lakhs, 650 square feet of land and 450 square feet of building area were purchased. What are the Specialties here?
An archway marks the entrance. Avenues with a tree canopy are a standard option. Throughout the week, security is available 24 hours a day, seven days a week. On each plot, there is a separate water pipeline. There will be paths for walking and running. Solar-powered street lighting is gaining popularity. The widths of the roads range from 60 to 40 feet.
